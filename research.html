Research
Silicon Photonic Circuits & Quantum Photonics
We develop reconfigurable silicon photonic circuits using liquid crystal-based phase modulators, targeting scalable quantum photonic computing platforms. Our research focuses on low-loss integration, dynamic phase control, and hybrid photonic architectures.
Liquid Crystal Electro-Optic Devices
We investigate advanced liquid crystal systems including polymer-stabilized liquid crystals (PSLC), ferronematic liquid crystals, and heliconical phases for high-performance electro-optic devices. Applications include tunable optical filters, modulators, and fast-response photonic components.
Liquid Crystal Elastomers & Soft Photonics
Our research explores liquid crystal elastomers as soft, responsive materials with coupled mechanical and optical properties. These systems enable novel photonic devices, soft actuators, and adaptive optical structures.
Computational Photonics & ML Optimization
We develop computational frameworks combining finite element methods and machine learning to accelerate photonic device design. Our work focuses on inverse design, optimization, and high-speed simulation of complex photonic systems.
