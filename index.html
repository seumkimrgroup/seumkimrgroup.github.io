Work with us

We welcome students and collaborators interested in materials, devices, and computational design.

Our research focuses on integrating liquid crystal materials with photonic platforms, enabling tunable optical devices and advanced system-level functionalities.

We are particularly interested in interdisciplinary approaches combining materials science, optical engineering, and computational optimization.
