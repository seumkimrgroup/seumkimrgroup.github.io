We recommend that undergraduate students apply in the second semester of the second year, or at the latest by the first semester of the third year.

학부연구생은 2학년 2학기 또는 늦어도 3학년 1학기에 지원하는 것을 권장합니다.

  • Research participation in experiments and simulations
  • Skill development through seminars and group meetings
  • Career exploration toward graduate study or industry

We recommend gaining at least one semester of research experience in the lab prior to applying for the M.S. program.

석사과정 진학 전 최소 한학기 이상 연구실에서 연구 경험을 쌓는 것을 권장합니다.

  • Research roadmap within a coherent topic category
  • Prior discussion recommended before formal application
  • Goal of publishing 2–3 SCI-indexed papers

Doctoral research involves long-term, challenging projects, and students are expected to maintain a high standard of research quality, independence, and originality.

박사과정에서는 매우 도전적인 연구를 수행하며 높은 수준의 연구 완성도와 창의성이 요구됩니다.

  • Independent research agenda and problem definition
  • High-level publications and conference presentations
  • Long-term contribution to original research directions
